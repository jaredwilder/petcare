Project PetCarePro

Software Requirements Specification

Version 1.0 Status: In Review

1. Introduction

This document outlines the requirements for Project PetCarePro, a unified web platform designed to replace the two existing company websites. The new platform will serve as the primary digital asset for informing potential franchisees, educating pet parents, and generating qualified business leads.

2. User Personas

Prospective Franchisee

An entrepreneur evaluating the franchise opportunity. Needs clear info on investment, support, and an easy way to inquire.

Pet Parent

A customer looking for local services, hours, and community content like events and photos.

Franchisee (Editor)

An owner who needs to log in and easily update their specific location's page with local news and events.

System Administrator

A technical user with full control to manage users, settings, and integrations across the entire site.

3. Specific Requirements

3.1 Functional Requirements

  • ✓User Roles: Granular permissions for Admins, Corporate, and Franchisees.
  • ✓Dynamic Content: Easy creation of Locations, Events, Contests, and Galleries.
  • ✓Lead Generation: Dedicated forms for franchise inquiries and customer contact.
  • ✓SEO: Auto XML sitemaps, customizable meta fields, and clean URLs.

3.2 Non-Functional Requirements

  • ✓Performance: Target Google PageSpeed score of 85+ on all devices.
  • ✓Usability: Minimalist, modern, mobile-first responsive design.
  • ✓Security: Full HTTPS encryption and protection against common vulnerabilities.
